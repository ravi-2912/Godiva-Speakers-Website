OUR STORY

Godiva Speakers is a popular public speaking and leadership skill development Club that helps individuls follow the Toastmasters' training program. The Club meets every 2nd, 4th and 5th Tuesday of the month in central Coventry.

Our mission is to help you build your communication skills, help you develop leadership abilities, boost your self confidence and help you find your voice.

At Godiva Speakers we pride ourselves on our friendliness and welcoming atmosphere. We want to see everyone become great public speakers and leaders, so we’ll do everything we can to help you achieve that goal.

The Godiva Speakers Club belongs to Toastmasters International. The International mission is to provide a mutually supportive environment where members can practice their leadership and speaking skills.

OUR VALUES

  • INTEGRITY

    Be honest and truthfull in our dealings and inter-actions with our fellow members and guests. Expect the same from others.

  • RESPECT

    Admire, recognise and appreciate our fellow members and guests, irrespective of difference in opinions. Respect all individual(s).

  • SERVICE

    Serve to help every member in our club to meet their personal and professional goals. Ensure being part of the support-system.

  • EXCELLENCE

    Be committed to the program, club meetings, core values and self development. Ensure members and guests obtain best experience.

What We Do

Experimental learning, peer feedback, mentoring, self paced program

PUBLIC SPEAKING

Using the Toastmasters International structured manual series, Godiva Speakers offers members opportunities to hone their public speaking and presentation skills in a lively and supportive environment.

LEADERSHIP

Using the Toastmasters International leadership guides, Godiva Speakers offers members opportunities to serve in meeting roles, as mentors, and as club officers, to improve leadership skills in a dynamic community of peers.

FEEDBACK

Using the Toastmasters International meeting and competition protocols, Godiva Speakers offers members opportunities to learn to give and receive essential feedback in impromptu speaking, communication, speech writing, and to develop intelligence.

NETWORKING

Within the Toastmasters International there are number of international and national events in which Godiva Speakers offers members opportunities to participate and thereby support a friendly, social and professional networking environmen.
